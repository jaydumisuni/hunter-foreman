# Phase 1 Demo Flow

## Judge Experience

The judge should understand Hunter Foreman in under three minutes.

## Flow

1. Customer submits a business request to ROSE.
2. ROSE collects the minimum required information.
3. ROSE hands a structured request to Foreman.
4. Foreman classifies intent and confidence.
5. Foreman selects a workflow.
6. A task appears on the dashboard.
7. Low-confidence or sensitive cases show human escalation.
8. Email and WhatsApp previews are generated.

## Demo Request Examples

### Event booking

A customer wants help setting up event invitations, tickets, and QR check-in.

### Tech support

A customer wants help with a website/app issue and needs the right person to follow up.

### Business automation

A customer wants an AI receptionist and workflow setup for their small business.

## Demo Promise

Hunter Foreman does not pretend to replace the business owner. It organizes work, reduces missed requests, and escalates when a human should decide.
